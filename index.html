About Eco Bites

Eco Bites is a technology-driven platform designed to reduce food waste and promote sustainability. Restaurants can report surplus food, and our logistics network collects and redistributes it through NGOs.

The platform is managed through one headquarters connected with seven regional driving hubs that coordinate pickups and deliveries.
